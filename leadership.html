Leadership
Governance & Escalation

Leadership exists to protect standards, consistency, and fairness. Escalation is not “snitching” — it’s how you keep the team clean and the server safe.

Command Center Certification

Escalate When

• The situation is sensitive (harassment, threats, underage, scams).

• You need higher permissions or leadership approval.

• The user disputes a decision and you have proof.

Always include proof + short context summary.

Never Do This

• Don’t argue in tickets.

• Don’t free-hand punishments with no documentation.

• Don’t promise roles to applicants.

• Don’t skip age verification.

Quality Standards

• Professional tone, every time.

• Fast routing (right person, right department).

• Clean closures + clear next steps.

Consistency is more important than speed.

Accountability

• Weekly performance minimums (tickets + messages).

• LOA required when inactive.

• Repeat issues → staff review.
