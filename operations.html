Module — Operations & Accountability

Mod Responsibilities & Commands

Operations exists for consistency. Use the correct commands, log sessions, and maintain weekly performance standards. Lack of logging or repeated failures triggers review.

Warn / Report Command

For minor rule violations, use:

*warn user-id reason (only in #staff-commands)

For major or repeated infractions:

/report with detailed documentation (proof, timestamps, context).

Shift Management

Begin every moderation session with:

/login

Conclude with:

/logout

Failure to log hours causes activity discrepancies and can result in disciplinary action.

LOA (Leave On Application)

If unavailable due to illness or personal circumstances, submit an LOA:

/loa with clear duration + reason.

Prior notification to senior staff is mandatory for extended absences.

Weekly Performance Metrics

Maintain minimum weekly thresholds to retain moderator status:

  • 10 Tickets — Process support tickets
  • 400 Messages — Active community engagement
  • Must use /login and /logout — Documented moderator actions

Performance below these standards triggers review and possible demotion.

Ticket Protocol Reminder

  • Professional greeting first (no slang in tickets)
  • Ask what they need / what role they’re applying for
  • Age verification is mandatory
  • Route to correct department + request proper proof
  • Document everything (especially escalations)
