Recruitment
Staff Intake Pipeline

This is the consistent process for recruiting new moderators and staff. Keep it structured. Keep it fair. Keep it documented.

Ticket Script Certification

Phase 1 — First Contact

• Have them open a ticket.

• Ask age (and confirm again).

• Ask motivation + availability.

• Evaluate communication + honesty.

Phase 2 — Screening

• 1–2 experience questions (if applicable).

• Ask if they’ll be loyal and consistent.

• If answers feel off → stop the process.

No rushing applicants just to fill slots.

Phase 3 — Training Route

• Send them to the Staff Academy site.

• Require reading every line.

• Then they take the exam.

Phase 4 — Finalization

• Review exam attempts (Admin logs).

• Assign role + explain expectations.

• Track performance weekly.
